♠ Royal Blackjack

HOW TO PLAY

Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Face cards are worth 10, Aces are 1 or 11. Blackjack (Ace + 10-value) pays 3:2.

ACTIONS

Hit — Draw another card. Stand — Keep your hand. Double — Double bet, get one card. Split — Split matching pairs. Surrender — Forfeit half your bet. Insurance — Insure vs dealer Ace.

SIDE BETS

Perfect Pairs — Bet your first two cards are a pair. 21+3 — Your two cards + dealer upcard make a poker hand.
